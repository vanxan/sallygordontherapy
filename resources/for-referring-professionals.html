For Professionals

Referring Clients to Sally Gordon Therapy

Information for pediatricians, family attorneys, social workers, and fellow therapists.

Thank You for Considering a Referral

Sally Gordon, LCSW, accepts referrals from healthcare providers, attorneys, social workers, educators, and fellow therapists for children, adults, and families who may benefit from neurofeedback and/or attachment therapy.

Sally's Specializations

  • Adoption, attachment, and developmental trauma — 30+ years of specialized experience
  • Neurofeedback (Othmer method) — For ADHD, anxiety, PTSD, sleep issues, migraines, and more
  • Dyadic Developmental Psychotherapy — Evidence-based attachment therapy
  • Attachment difficulties — Assessment and treatment of disordered attachment patterns

Credentials

  • Licensed Clinical Social Worker (LCSW) — NC License #C001967
  • NC Court-Qualified Expert Witness in adoption, attachment, and trauma
  • Former trainer, Jordan Institute for Families at UNC-Chapel Hill School of Social Work
  • Othmer Method Neurofeedback Certified
  • Trained in Dyadic Developmental Psychotherapy and Trauma Resiliency Model
  • 30+ years of clinical experience

Good Candidates for Referral

  • Adopted children with behavioral or emotional difficulties
  • Children or adults with ADHD who want non-medication options
  • Trauma survivors (children or adults) who haven't responded fully to talk therapy
  • Families struggling with attachment and bonding issues
  • Clients with anxiety, depression, or sleep issues interested in neurofeedback

How to Refer

Simply have your client call (704) 215-4598 or email sally@sallygordontherapy.com to schedule a free phone consultation. Sally is happy to coordinate care and communicate with referring providers as needed.

For questions about a specific case or to discuss whether a client may be appropriate for referral, Sally welcomes direct calls from professionals.
